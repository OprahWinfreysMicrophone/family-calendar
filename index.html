For T, M, and C — Adding Events

  1. Tap + Add Event above
  2. Choose the type (Family or Doctor)
  3. Fill in the name, date, and any notes for G
  4. Tap Save to Family Calendar
  5. G will see it immediately when they open the app

For G — Viewing Appointments

  1. Tap your name (G) on the first screen
  2. Use the arrows to go forward or back in months
  3. Each card shows the date, time, and any notes
  4. Red cards = doctor visits  |  Green cards = family events

Bookmark this page

  1. On a phone: tap the share button, then "Add to Home Screen"
  2. On a computer: press Ctrl+D (or Cmd+D on Mac)
  3. It will always show the latest events — no refresh needed
